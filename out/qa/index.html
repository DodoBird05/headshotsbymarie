What should I wear for my photo session?

By Marie Feutrier

Wear something that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Solid colors work best, and avoid busy patterns. I recommend bringing a few outfit options so we can choose what photographs best.

How long does a typical session take?

Professional headshot sessions typically take 1-2 hours. This gives us plenty of time to try different angles, backgrounds, and expressions without feeling rushed.

When will I receive my photos?

You will receive your fully edited photos within 7-10 business days after your session. Rush delivery is available upon request.
